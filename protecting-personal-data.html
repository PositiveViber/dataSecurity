Understanding Personal Data Risks

Personal data risks are everywhere. Learn how to protect yourself with these tools and tips.


Discover online courses and materials to understand cyber threats and learn protective measures. Knowledge is the first line of defense!

Adopt strong password hygiene, enable multi-factor authentication, and be vigilant about the information you share on social platforms.

Explore antivirus software, VPNs, and password managers to bolster your digital defenses.

Stay up-to-date with the latest software patches. Regular updates can significantly reduce vulnerability to attacks.

Ensure that your data is regularly backed up to avoid loss during unexpected cyber incidents.


Tools and Resources

Equip yourself with the best tools to keep your personal information out of the wrong hands.


Master cybersecurity fundamentals through Coursera's Cybersecurity Specialization or explore Khan Academy's Cryptography for a deeper understanding of data protection. Knowledge is your first line of defense!

Strengthen your digital security by enabling two-factor authentication on your accounts and learning about safe password practices from the FTC.

Enhance your digital defenses with LastPass password manager, Malwarebytes anti-malware, and secure your connections with NordVPN.

Minimize vulnerabilities by regularly updating your devices. The Australian Cyber Security Centre provides insights on why keeping software up-to-date is crucial.

Protect against data loss by following the U.S. government's advice on data backup. Consider cloud services like Dropbox or Google Drive for easy access and security.
